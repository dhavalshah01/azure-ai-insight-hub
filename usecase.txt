MACU is focused on building a secure, observable, and scalable AI platform using a combination of Application Insights, APIM, and AI Gateway. For observability, they want full end-to-end tracing of their agent and RAG pipeline through Application Insights, ideally configured via IaC (Bicep/Terraform) rather than the portal, with logs forwarded to New Relic via Event Hub to integrate with their existing SRE tooling.  On the APIM and AI Gateway side, they are looking to centralize LLM access governance — replacing ad hoc deployments with a hub model that handles semantic caching, token-based rate limiting, multi-region load balancing across PTU and PAYG endpoints, keyless access, and multi-tenancy via APIM policies — all while maintaining full observability from a single control plane. This is scoped as a dedicated Solution Optimization workshop session, and they have an open need for a CSA SME on APIM/AI Gateway to support the implementation as they scale from ~100 to 3,800 internal users.
